1. Project Overview

The AtliQ Hotels Business Insights project transforms raw operational data into actionable intelligence via a Power BI dashboard. Covering multiple properties across India, it tracks Revenue, ADR, RevPAR, Occupancy Rate, DSRN, and Realization Percentage to empower strategic decisions.

2. Data Model & Star Schema

Built on a star schema, the data model includes two fact tables (Revenue, Bookings) and three dimensions (Hotels, Dates, Customer Segments). This structure optimizes query performance and supports flexible slicing and dicing of KPIs.

3. Dashboard Visualization

The dashboard features interactive slicers for date ranges, hotel locations, and customer segments. Dynamic tooltips reveal underlying data, while trend charts and KPI cards highlight key performance metrics at a glance.

4. Key Insights & Recommendations

  • Flat ADR Trends: Lack of demand-based pricing led to stagnant Average Daily Rate.
  • Dynamic Pricing Potential: Implementing weekday/weekend rate differentials can boost ADR and overall revenue.
  • Property Performance: Atliq Grands Bangalore & Seasons Mumbai show lowest occupancy and highest cancellations, suggesting review of service quality or pricing strategy.
  • Strategic Recommendation: Maintain uniform pricing across OTAs; offer exclusive direct-booking discounts to drive direct revenue and reduce commission costs.

6. Deployment

The solution is deployed via Power BI Service, enabling stakeholders to access reports securely in the cloud. Role-based access ensures data governance and compliance.

7. Future Enhancements

  • Predictive Analytics: Integrate machine learning models to forecast occupancy and demand.
  • Mobile-Optimized Reports: Enhance layouts for on-the-go decision making.
  • Alerting System: Real-time notifications for KPI thresholds (e.g., sharp ADR drops).
  • Natural Language Q&A: Allow conversational insights via Power BI Q&A feature.
