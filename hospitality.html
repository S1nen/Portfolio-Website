Hospitality business insights – AtliQ Hotels

This project delivers an in-depth analysis of hotel performance data for the Atliq Group, which operates multiple properties across various cities in India. The dashboard is designed to empower stakeholders with data-driven insights into key operational metrics, helping them make informed decisions around revenue optimization and resource management. Built on a robust star schema data model, the solution includes two fact tables (revenue and bookings) and three dimension tables (hotels, dates, and customer segments), ensuring efficient querying and seamless data exploration. The dashboard tracks critical KPIs such as Revenue, Average Daily Rate (ADR), Revenue per Available Room (RevPAR), Occupancy Rate, Daily Sold Room Nights (DSRN), and Realization Percentage. With interactive filters, slicers, and dynamic tooltips, users can analyze performance trends across time, location, customer types, and more, all within a visually engaging and intuitive interface.

The project also dives deep into pricing strategies and behavioral trends to identify revenue opportunities. Weekday vs. weekend performance and demand-based pricing patterns are analyzed to guide dynamic pricing decisions that can maximize returns. Additionally, occupancy trends and cancellation patterns are examined to highlight areas of improvement, such as overbooking strategies or targeted promotions during low-demand periods. The insights gained from this dashboard enable the Atliq Group to fine-tune their business strategies, minimize revenue leakage, and enhance overall hotel performance. Ultimately, this solution serves as a comprehensive tool for aligning operational efforts with financial goals across their hotel portfolio.

Project Components:

  1. Data Modeling & Star Schema Design
  2. Data Visualization & KPI Analysis
  3. Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations

Data Analysis & Key Insights

  • Hotels were not using demand-based pricing, leading to flat ADR trends.
  • Introducing dynamic pricing (weekday/weekend) can boost ADR & revenue.
  • Atliq Grands Bangalore & Atliq Seasons Mumbai had the lowest occupancy & highest cancellation rates, indicating possible service/pricing issues.
  • Strategy Recommendation: Maintain uniform pricing across platforms (MakeMyTrip, Agoda) & introduce exclusive direct booking discount coupons.
