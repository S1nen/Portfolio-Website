Hospitality Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Dashboard

This Power BI project analyzes hotel performance data from the Atliq Group, covering multiple properties across cities in India. The interactive dashboard provides data-driven insights into revenue trends, occupancy rates, pricing strategies, and cancellations, helping optimize business decision-making and revenue management.

Key Highlights: Star Schema Implementation with 2 Fact Tables & 3 Dimension Tables KPIs Tracked: Revenue, ADR, RevPAR, Occupancy%, DSRN, and Realization% Dynamic Insights: Interactive Filters, Slicers, & Tooltips for trend analysis Pricing Strategy Optimization: Weekday/Weekend & Demand-based Pricing Analysis Occupancy & Cancellation Analysis: Identified patterns to improve revenue streams

Project Components:

  1. Data Modeling & Star Schema Design
  2. Data Visualization & KPI Analysis
  3. Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations
  • Data Schema: Structured in a Star Schema for optimal performance.

Data Analysis & Key Insights

  • Hotels were not using demand-based pricing, leading to flat ADR trends.
  • Introducing dynamic pricing (weekday/weekend) can boost ADR & revenue.
  • Atliq Grands Bangalore & Atliq Seasons Mumbai had the lowest occupancy & highest cancellation rates, indicating possible service/pricing issues.
  • Strategy Recommendation: Maintain uniform pricing across platforms (MakeMyTrip, Agoda) & introduce exclusive direct booking discount coupons.

Interactive Power BI Dashboard

  • Comprehensive Visualization of Hotel Performance Metrics.
  • Dynamic Filters & Slicers for deeper insights.
  • Revenue Optimization Strategies & Cancellation Analysis.
