Sales Insights – Computer Hardware Business

An interactive BI dashboard uncovering revenue, trends, and customer behavior for AtliQ Hardware.

View Live Dashboard

1. Project Overview

The Sales Insights dashboard provides a comprehensive view of AtliQ Hardware’s performance. Stakeholders can explore trends, top products, regional stats, and customer behavior to drive strategic decisions and revenue growth.

2. ETL & Data Model

Data was extracted from MySQL, transformed with SQL scripts, and modeled into a star schema:

  • Fact Sales: Transaction-level data
  • Dim Products: Product names and categories
  • Dim Date: Date/time hierarchy
  • Dim Region: Regional classification

3. Dashboard Visualization

Includes slicers by region, time, and product. Visuals include sales trend lines, bar charts for best sellers, map views, and KPI cards for revenue, YoY growth, and customer stats.

4. Key Insights & Metrics

  • Seasonal Trends: Sales peaked during launch and holiday months
  • Top Products: GPUs and SSDs contributed 30%+ of total revenue
  • Regional Performance: West leads in AOV; East shows growth potential
  • Customer Behavior: Repeat buyers have 15% higher order value
  • Growth Target: Dashboard supports a 7% projected revenue increase
