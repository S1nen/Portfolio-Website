1. Project Overview

The Sales Insights dashboard for AtliQ Hardware provides a comprehensive view of sales data, enabling stakeholders to track revenue trends, identify top-performing products, and analyze regional and customer purchase behaviors. Designed to support a targeted revenue growth of at least 7% next quarter, it delivers actionable intelligence through intuitive visuals.

2. ETL & Data Model

Data is sourced from the company’s SQL database and processed via ETL routines that clean, transform, and load sales records into a star schema optimized for reporting. Key fact and dimension tables include:

  • Fact Sales: Transaction-level sales data.
  • Dim Products: Product categories and attributes.
  • Dim Date: Date hierarchy for time-series analysis.
  • Dim Region: Geographic segmentation.

3. Dashboard Visualization

The dashboard features dynamic filters for time period, product category, and region. Visual elements include line charts for sales trends, bar charts for top products, map visuals for regional distribution, and KPI cards for quick metrics like total revenue and growth percentage.

4. Key Insights & Metrics

  • Seasonal Trends: Identified peak sales periods during product launch months and holiday seasons.
  • Top Products: Certain hardware categories (e.g., GPUs, SSDs) consistently contribute over 30% of revenue.
  • Regional Performance: Western region reports highest average order value; Eastern region shows growth potential.
  • Customer Behavior: Repeat customers purchase 40% more frequently and have a 15% higher average order value.
  • Revenue Growth: Dashboard insights support strategies to achieve a projected 7% uplift in the next quarter.

6. Deployment

Deployed to Power BI Service with role-based access controls. Scheduled data refresh ensures daily updates, keeping insights current and reliable for decision-making.
