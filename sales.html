Sales Insights – Computer hardware business

This project focuses on the design and development of an interactive BI dashboard for AtliQ Hardware, a company specializing in computer hardware products. The goal of the dashboard is to analyze sales data comprehensively and provide actionable insights that can drive data-informed decision-making and support revenue growth. Leveraging SQL for data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL), the project ensures clean and well-structured data is available for analysis. The dashboard incorporates dynamic filtering options and slicers, allowing users to explore sales metrics across various dimensions such as time period, product category, and region. Through intuitive visualizations, the dashboard highlights sales trends over time, enabling users to identify seasonal fluctuations, emerging patterns, and shifts in market demand.

Key areas of analysis include product performance, customer purchase behavior, and regional sales distribution. The dashboard evaluates which product categories and individual hardware items contribute the most to revenue, helping s takeholders prioritize inventory and marketing strategies. Regional insights offer a clear picture of where sales are strongest geographically, enabling targeted campaigns and resource allocation. Customer analytics uncover patterns in buying behavior, such as purchase frequency and product preferences, supporting better customer engagement and retention strategies. The insights generated from this solution aim to support a targeted revenue growth goal of at least 7% in the upcoming quarter by aligning sales strategies with real-time data and business intelligence.

Project Components:

  1. Data Modeling & Star Schema Design
  2. Data Visualization & KPI Analysis
  3. Business Insights & Strategic Recommendations

Data Analysis & Key Insights

  • Data Source: SQL database containing sales records of AtliQ Hardware.
  • Data Cleaning & Transformation: Performed using SQL queries to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
  • Visualization & Reporting: Power BI was used to create dynamic dashboards with interactive visual elements.
  • KPIs & Metrics: Revenue trends, sales growth percentage, top-selling products, sales by region, and profit margins.
